Who I am

About me

Hey there, I'm Leonhard! I'm a frontend developer with a passion for good looking user-friendly interfaces, which simplifies user's lives and cuts unnecessary chaos. I love Coding reladet stuff, because I can practice my creativity, understanding systems do problem solving. Basicly I find solutions and improve them afterwords, like an architect or engineer.

I'm currently based in Austria/Innsbruck, but I'm open for remote work or relocation with almost no limit.

Improving my Skills is standard in IT, but I'm seriously addicted to learning new things. Especially in the AI Aria, I want to know how LLM's work and the list goes on but this container here is to small for that.

The exiting part of my job as a Frontend Developer is problem-solving and finding solutions for various problems. My approach is mostly look for at least two perspektives to solve a problem and decide which has a good afect/komplexity ratio.
